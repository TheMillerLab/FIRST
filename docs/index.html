FIRST Study

Frontline Immunotherapy with Response-Guided Subsequent Treatment

This site provides supplemental materials, model-building workflows, and reproducible Bayesian analyses supporting the FIRST study, an institutional cohort study of frontline immunotherapy for high-risk cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (CSCC).

Materials include generative modeling workflows, prior and posterior checks, convergence diagnostics, and model-specific analysis documents corresponding to the primary objective response and event-free survival analyses.

Core Workflows

  • Response Modeling Workflow
    Generative Bayesian modeling workflow for objective response, including prior specification, simulation-based validation, posterior predictive checks, and dose-response model development.
  • Event-Free Survival Workflow
    Generative workflow for landmark event-free survival modeling, including prior predictive simulation, Weibull survival modeling, and sensitivity analyses.

Model-Specific Analyses

  • ORR Model: Linear Dose Specification
    Full model-specific analysis for Bayesian objective response modeling under a linear dose parameterization.
  • ORR Model: Log-Dose Specification
    Model-specific analysis for Bayesian objective response modeling under a log-dose formulation allowing diminishing returns.
  • ORR Model: Categorical Dose Specification
    Model-specific analysis for Bayesian objective response modeling using threshold-based categorical dose terms.
  • Event-Free Survival Model
    Full model-specific analysis for the landmark Bayesian event-free survival model and associated sensitivity analyses.

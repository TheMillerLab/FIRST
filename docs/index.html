Supplemental Materials

FIRST Study

Frontline Immunotherapy with Response-Guided Subsequent Treatment

This site provides supplemental materials, model-building workflows, and reproducible Bayesian analyses supporting the FIRST study, an institutional cohort study of frontline immunotherapy for high-risk cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (CSCC).

Materials include generative modeling workflows, prior and posterior checks, convergence diagnostics, and model-specific analysis documents corresponding to the primary objective response and event-free survival analyses.
